Privacy Policy

1. Who We Are

DATAEX is a sole proprietorship that publishes downloadable and remotely-accessible software. We sell through FastSpring, which acts as the Merchant of Record (MoR) for our transactions.

2. What We Collect

(a) Website data: limited technical data your browser sends (IP address, device/OS, pages viewed, timestamps).

(b) Purchase data: order details (product, price, currency, tax, date), license/activation data, and contact details you provide at checkout (name, email, company). Payment card data is processed by FastSpring; we do not receive or store your full card number.

(c) Support communications: messages you send us (including email address and any files you attach).

3. How We Use Information

  • To fulfill orders, deliver license keys, and provide updates.
  • To respond to support requests.
  • To secure, maintain, and improve the website and software.

4. Sharing

We share information with service providers only as needed to operate (e.g., FastSpring for checkout and taxes; hosting and email providers). We do not sell personal information.

5. Cookies / Analytics

Our website uses only the cookies necessary to operate pages and downloads. If we later add analytics, we will update this Policy.

6. Data Retention

We retain order records and license data for as long as required for accounting, tax, and security, then delete or anonymize them.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the nature of the data.

8. Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your personal information by contacting us. We may need to retain certain records for legal reasons.

9. Children

10. International

11. FastSpring

FastSpring is Merchant of Record and handles tax calculation, collection, and remittance, and payment processing. Please refer to FastSpring’s publicly available tax and privacy materials for more information.
