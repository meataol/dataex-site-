About DATAEX

DATAEX is a Windows tool for Excel users who need to extract and consolidate data from many files quickly and reliably— without writing scripts. Define rules (fixed cells, ranges, filters), scan entire folders including subfolders, and export a clean Excel report.

The application runs locally on your PC; your files remain on your machine. Checkout and taxes are handled by FastSpring (Merchant of Record).
